Task 2

Explain why some variable names in the example are acceptable and why some are not in Python

CG T2

Write your answer here for sample variable names

Valid variable names are acceptable because they:

1) begin with a letter or underscore,

2) contain only letters, digits, or underscores,

3) do not include spaces or special characters.

The examples, grade_test, covid_19_cases, term2, Sale_price_2021, and miva_learners follow all these rules.

Invalid variable names violate Python's rules. Examples include test(grade) (parentheses), covid-19-cases (hyphens), 2 term or 2021sales_price (starting with numbers), and miva&learners (ampersand).
